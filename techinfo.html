Benue Tech Skills

Benue Tech skills also known as “Benue ICT Ecosystem” initiative is designed to empower Benue State youths with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel and dominate in digital economy and foster economic growth and innovation within and outside the state.

In an effort to create employment opportunities for youths, the Benue State government under the leadership of the state Govenor Rev Fr Hyacinth Alia Benue is collaborating with Troika Tex Digital Communications Ltd., BishBerry Digital Solutions Ltd., Google and Microsoft to conduct a comprehensive tech skills for youth

The programme holds immense importance in today's digital age. As technology evolves, the industries shape the job markets. It is crucial for Benue youths to be equipped with the skills required to thrive in a digital environment. By providing specialized training in digital marketing & e-commerce, advanced data analytics, data science & analysis, UI/UX, frontend development, and backend development. The training is aimed at bridging the digital skills gap in Benue State and to ensure that the youths are well-prepared for the future of work.

Benue Tech Skills is aim at training and empowering youth in ICT for a period of 6 month.
